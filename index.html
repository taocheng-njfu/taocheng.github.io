Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 2026-02-01

This Privacy Policy applies to the Dailything mobile application developed by I.

The App is a completely offline personal diary. This policy is intended to inform you about my practices regarding the handling of your information.

1. No Data Collection

The App does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data or information from your device. This includes, but is not limited to:

  • Diary Entries & Photos: All text, images, and any media you create within the App are stored exclusively on your own device.
  • Location Data: The App does not access, collect, or use your geographical location.
  • Device Information: The App does not collect your device's IP address, unique identifiers, operating system version, or usage analytics.
  • Personal Identifiers: I do not require you to provide any email address, name, or other personally identifiable information to use the App.

Since no data is collected, there is also no sharing of data with any third parties.

2. Local Storage & Your Control

All your data (diary entries, settings, etc.) are saved locally on your device's storage. They are never automatically sent to any server or cloud service (including iCloud or Google Drive) unless you manually and explicitly choose to export or back them up using your device's built-in share or file system functions.

You have full control over your data. You can delete all App data at any time by uninstalling the App from your device.

3. Children's Privacy

The App is safe for users of all ages as it does not collect any information. I do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 (or the minimum age in your country).

I may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.

5. Your Consent

By using the App, you signify your understanding and acceptance of the practices described in this Privacy Policy.
